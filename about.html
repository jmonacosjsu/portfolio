I've found that this path is perfect for someone like me, someone who is always asking questions, loves spending time on projects, and always looking for small ways to improve. I've recently completed the Google Data Analytics Certificate and capstone project and I am currently enrolled in the Information Science and Data Analytics program at SJSU.
